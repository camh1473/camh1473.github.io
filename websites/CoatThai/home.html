Coat & Thai, curb-side kitchen.

Welcome to Coat and Thai, Austin's best authentic Thai restaurant. Founded in 2010 by Owner/Chef Ninrat with fresh ingredients and authenticity in mind. Coat and Thai is located at The Picnic Food Truck Park on Barton Springs Road, and has been named Best Thai Food Trailer in Austin by Lazy Smurf's Guide to Life.
